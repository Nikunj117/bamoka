Storage : Fresh and Protected Products

Guar gum is sensitive to environmental changes, so we prioritize meticulous packaging and storage to maintain its stability and prevent clumping or degradation. Our storage facilities are designed to control temperature and humidity levels, ensuring optimal conditions for our guar gum powder.

We employ modern inventory management techniques that enable us to process orders efficiently and reduce storage times. This commitment ensures that our products are delivered promptly, minimizing waiting times for our customers.

Some key points that should be considered while purchasing and keeping Guar Gum packaging safe:

  • Guar gum powder should be stored in a cool, dry place, away from heat and sunlight.
  • It is advisable to consume the powder within a reasonable time after opening the bag.
  • Prolonged exposure to open air can lead to moisture absorption, resulting in microbiological degradation due to the hygroscopic nature of guar gum powder.
  • The properties of guar gum powder remain stable for 12 to 18 months. Shelf life may be extended by adding suitable preservatives.
