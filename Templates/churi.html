Guar Gum Churi

Guar Gum Churi
Product Info

One useful by-product of the guar gum extraction process is Guar Gum Churi. Usually, the guar seeds are ground and crushed to extract the guar gum, which results in Guar Churi Powder. After that, whatever remains is treated to get rid of imperfections and guarantee a consistent-quality final product. It is an affordable, high-protein feed ingredient that may be incorporated into a variety of animal feed recipes.

Benefits of Guar Gum Churi:
  • High Protein Content: Guar Gum Churi with its 40% protein content, offers animals a substantial supply of vital amino acids. This promotes the growth of muscles, milk production, and overall animal health.
  • Increased Digestibility: Animals’ ability to digest protein and amino acids is improved by breaking down the guar seed’s cell walls during the roasting process. As a result, animals are able to absorb more nutrients from their Guar, and their growth and performance are enhanced.
  • Cost-effective: Guar gum Churi is far less expensive than other protein sources, such as soya. This makes it a practical choice for farmers who want to get the most out of their feed.
  • Versatility: Guar gum churi is suitable for use in a wide range of animal feed formulations, including as feed for fish, poultry, cattle, and pigs. It can be combined with other feed ingredients to make a balanced and nutrient-rich diet for animals, or it can be used as a complete source of protein.
  • Good Source of Carbohydrates: A substantial quantity of carbohydrates are also present in guar gum churi, which gives animals energy and supports their intestinal health.
Applications of Guar Gum Churi:
  • Cattle Feed: Enhances overall well-being and milk productivity.
  • Poultry feed: Promotes growth and egg production.
  • Fish feed: Gives fish the vital nutrients they need to stay healthy.
  • Swine feed: Encourages the development of lean meat and quick growth.
  • Pet Food: Boosts pet food’s nutritious content.
When choosing Guar Meal Churi for your animal feed,
several factors should be considered:
  • Animal species: Different animals have different appetites and nutritional requirements. Choose Guar Gum Churi whose protein content is appropriate for the animals you care for.
  • Quality: Make sure you purchase the Organic Guar Churi from a reputable supplier, such as Bamoka Industries, and the source you choose complies with strict quality control guidelines.
  • Cost: Examine or compare rates offered by various vendors of Guar Gum Churi to determine the best value for money. Bamoka Industries can offer the most affordable Guar Gum products without compromising the quality.
